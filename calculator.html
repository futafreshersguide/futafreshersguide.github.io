1. Admission Aggregate

How it works: FUTA uses a 75:25 screening ratio. Your UTME score is weighted at 75%, and your 5 core O'Level subjects are weighted at 25%. This is an unofficial estimation.

2. Semester GPA

How it works: GPA is calculated by dividing Total Credit Points (TCP) by Total Load Units (TLU). Ensure you capture the correct unit load for every course.

3. Cumulative CGPA

Crucial Rule: Never calculate CGPA by finding the average of your GPAs. You must sum all your historical Total Credit Points (TCP) and divide by historical Total Load Units (TLU).
